Monica Lyn

Co-founder, President

Monica Lyn (she/her/hers) is a spoiled daughter, imperfect sister, loving partner, fearless friend, and space holder for queer people of color.
While spending much of her adult life navigating and being re-traumatized by PWIs in higher education, she walked away with some degrees, professional licenses, and a deeply profound understanding that healing for Black and Brown folx was unlikely to be discovered in a textbook.
Presently, she is striving to be a better human by working on her own healing and decolonization, destigmatizing mental wellness within Black and Brown communities, and dismantling the notion that traditional psychotherapy is the only/right/correct path to mental wellness.
Healing can come in many beautiful modalities and while therapy is her lane, she ain't arguing with indigenous ancestral wisdom.
