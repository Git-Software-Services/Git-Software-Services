WHO WE ARE

We focus on providing our members with a collection of psychedelic content by the BIPOC community.

We catalog a collection of video and audio content as well as other resources to help our members stay connected to each other as well as other BIPOC psychedelic explorers and educators.

We saw a need for a collection of experiences that feature and honor our community. Melanated Consciousness is the one place on the internet that you can go to browse through dozens of videos that highlight us sharing our stories and experiences with various psychedelics.
