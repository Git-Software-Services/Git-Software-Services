The Black Consciousness Collective

Community | Healing | Transformation

TBCC is a collection of Experiences Resources Information created by and for BIPOC interested in psychedelics.

This is an inclusive space where people can share their stories with honesty and vulnerability.

Our Mission

To inform BIPOC on the therapeutic and spiritual roots of psychedelics, harm reduction, and overall psychedelic usage.

Our Vision

To highlight BIPOC’s contributions to the field of psychedelics. We want to craft a narrative by and for BIPOC and make it available to the community.

Explore The Black Consciousness Collection

Members have access to a compilation of information on etheogenics, including shared personal experiences from other BIPOC in the form of audio uploads, video recordings, documentation links and virtual meetings .

Meet the Founders Family Friends of TBCC

Months before my first experience — I searched the web, consuming any media that featured psychedelic trip reports. When I would stumble across a person of color bold enough to share their experience with the world — I saved the link. More important to me than what the world had to say about psychedelics was what the BIPOC community were saying! Over time that list has grown to become the foundation for TBCC.

Dorian Wallace —CEO
