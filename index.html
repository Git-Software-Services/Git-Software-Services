Melanated Consciousness

Melanated | Psychedelic | Community

We center the lived experiences of BIQTPOC Black Indigenous Queer Trans People of Color folx.

Black, Indigenous, Queer, Trans, People of Color are exploring psychedelics/Entheogenics/Plant Medicine and sharing their experiences with honesty and vulnerability. We find these hidden jewels and share them with you!

Our Mission

To provide insight for our members by making any and all psychedelic content available by BIQTPOC. To provide highly recommended resources along with affordable and accessible integration for our communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where Truth shines so brightly we will no longer need to try and describe it with words. We will know that our Truth, without words, is reflected in each other.
