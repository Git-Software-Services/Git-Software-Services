Melanated Consciousness

Melanated | Psychedelic | Community

We center the lived experiences of BIPOC Black Indigenous People of Color folx.

Black, Indigenous, People of Color are exploring psychedelics/Entheogenics/Plant Medicine and sharing their experiences with honesty and vulnerability. We find these hidden jewels and share them with you!

Our Mission

To provide insight for our members by making available as many psychedelic trip reports as we possibly can!

Our Vision

We envision a world where Truth shines so brightly we will no longer need to try and describe it with words.

Explore Melanated Consciousness

Members have access to a compilation of information on etheogenics, including trip reports as well as psychedelic information as it pertains to race in the form of audio uploads, video recordings, and additional resources .

Meet the Founders Family Friends of Melanated Consciousness

Months before my first experience — I searched the web, consuming any media that featured psychedelic trip reports. When I would stumble across a person of color bold enough to share their experience with the world — I saved the link. More important to me than what the world had to say about psychedelics was what the BIPOC community was saying! Over time that list has grown to become the foundation for Melanated Consciousness.

Dorian Wallace —CEO
