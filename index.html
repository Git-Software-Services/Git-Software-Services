The Black Consciousness Collective

Community | Healing | Transformation

TBCC is a collection of Experiences Resources Information created by and for Black folx interested in psychedelics.

This is an inclusive space where people can share their stories with honesty and vulnerability.

Our Mission

To educate people of color on the therapeutic and spiritual roots of psychedelics, harm reduction, and overall drug policy. To provide psychedelic education to historically marginalized communities in order to foster empowerment and healing.

Our Vision

To highlight people of colorâ€™s contributions to the field of psychedelics. We want to craft a narrative by and for people of color by elevating experts and policy makers who themselves are people of color.

Members have access to a compilation of information on etheogenics, including shared personal experiences from other people of color in the form of audio uploads, video recordings, documentation links and virtual meetings .
