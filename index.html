Integration Therapy : Integrating Your Psychedelic Experience

We do not provide or condone the use of illegal substances or any other illegal activities.

Integration Therapy is designed to support synthesis of the mind and body following the experience of a non-ordinary state of consciousness prompted by ingestion of psychedelics. Though psychedelic experiences are highly regulated within the US, many people continue to seek out psychedelics within certain clinical, religious, and/or spiritual settings to expand consciousness, change behavior, process trauma, and embark on healing journeys. Therapy for Queer People of Color has an amazing therapist directory where you can find qualified professionals who provide psychedelic integration therapy. Integration plans can be as simple as a one time appointment to process a single experience or several appointments to work through more complex psychedelic journeys. Schedule a consultation today to get started.

Meet the Founders Family Friends of Melanated Consciousness

Our target demographic is the BIPOC community. Integration is a big part of what we offer but the main focus is addressing the mounting accessibility issues that are preventing our communities from experiencing these healing modalities. The stigmas and barriers to healing for BIPOC communities are predominately systemic. This platform is about building trust and allowing a safe space to learn about and explore psychedelics with other BIPOC individuals.
