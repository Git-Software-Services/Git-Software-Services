MAY 2020

My First Bufo Experience

By Dorian Wallace

About a month prior to this Bufo experience, I had an experience with DMT that really shook things up.

I had my my wife, at the time, do a tarot reading for me. Because up to that point I'd been nervous about attempting DMT. I'd watched so many of the videos that are now on this platform and I thought I had some idea what I could expect, but fear was still a factor. The card reading was exactly what I needed to take the plunge. I sat on the edge of the bed and pulled in the three required inhalations of the DMT. By the secoond pull I was positive I didn't need a third pull, but I did it anyway because I knew it was very likely that I'd only have this experience once and I knew that I wanted to break through. On the second pull, the room I was in barely resembled what you see in the image below. Everything was still there, but it felt and looked like I was in a temple. The only words that come close to describing where I was after that third pull are "holy", "reverant", "Ancient Egypt", "heavenly". I know that Egypt is a weird word to throw in there, especially considering I've never been, but that descriptor still holds true, as I write these words nearly a year later.

I won't go into the entire experience of breaking through. I do dive a bit deeper into my DMT experience in the Bufo Ceremony Description video below.

I posted the image below of the plant because as I was coming out of the experience, that plant in the picture was alive! It was moving and flowing. I really wished that I would have thought to touch it. But I was so in awe of everything that I had just experienced and was still experiencing. It was so incredibly amazing...

I was shown the Truth about myself and following that experience one of the decisions that I made was to leave my partner of seven years. Following that, I rented an airbnb in Atlanta for a month, and tried to figure things out. I felt like I was being guided but I had no idea where I was going or what I was supposed to do. I spent that time alone meditating, walking and processing that DMT experience. It was so profound! I had no idea experiences like that were possible on this planet.

A couple of weeks went by and one day I watched a video of a woman having a very beautiful bufo experience. I knew what bufo was because it had come up in my research of DMT. I knew that it was meant for me to experience one day but until that moment I had not considered that it could happen so soon. In the comments of the video that I watched, someone asked about where they could have an experience like this and the woman responded with the website information. I reached out to the organization and found out that the next retreat would be in less than 10 days and only 2 hours away from me. I immediately booked the retreat.

I want to preface the video by saying that the four people who went before me had very calm and quiet experiences —although profound, and I expected mine to be the same. As a matter of fact, I was feeling very out of my element and annoyed that this retreat was a camping retreat and taking place outside in the woods. There were ticks all over everybody, someone had recently spotted poison ivy where we were all sitting, I had been too cold to get enough sleep the night before because I was sleeping in a tent —without a blanket, and I was around a whole bunch of hippie white peoople who all seemed to pretty much know each other. Basically, I just wanted to hurry up and have my experience with Bufo and figure out how to get out of there as quickly as possible.

What the video doesn't show is what happened inside of me... something that I can't even really put into words. Within five seconds of breathing in the medicine and holding it, It was like everything and everyone around me disappeared. There was only me in the entire universe. I was the universe. This realization at first startled me and then I felt fear. Then the illusion of control vanished and I let go. I felt waves of intense sound and energy washing over me. It was happening so fast that it was like a strobe pulsing. I can't describe what I saw but it was an opportunity to release my grasp on all the crap that I'd been holding onto. All the trauma, shame, self-doubt, caring what other people thought of me, beliefs that didn't serve me, holding myself back.... all of that dead weight that I'd been carrying, along with the root of why I'd been carrying it.... was being released.

I felt as if all of my Chakras were opened and that negative energy that kept me unbalanced and kept me from being me just shot out of me. I don't raise my voice and definitely don't raise it around a bunch of strangers. In those moments I was aware of their presence but I didn't care, I just let it all out of me. I freed myself! After that experience I felt so much lighter, freer and so much more myself than I've ever felt. Before I lived my life as if I was always being observed, always trying to do what I thought was the right thing, but for who? Not me. The real me had become buried under everyone else's expectation and I didn't know how to escape that. Within minutes I burst free and it felt like breathing. It felt like living! I looked around at everyone and I could feel their genuine happiness for my freedom. It was an incredible experience. It was the reset that I needed.

This video shows the first ceremony with bufo, but I actually ended up doing it a second time, two days later at the same retreat.

So much has happened in my life since that first bufo experience. I understood after that the amount of self-work and healing that was needed. Bufo released so much that was inside of me that needed to come out, but I also became aware of all the rest of it.

I do not believe that these healing modalities are the shortcut to enlightenment but I do think that they offer tremendous insight into who we are and our purpose. They allow us to get out of our own way so that we can live lives that are more fulfilling because we face our fears and the Truth and understand that they are the same. By choosing to have these experiences we begin to know experientially as opposed to conceptually, that we are the creators of our own experiences and that lived knowingness is freedom.

I drop a lot of F-bombs in this video. I think that word came in really handy in enabling me to express to the best of my ability what I'd just experienced. It's taken me a very long time to make this video public. It's an extremely vulnerable experience but one that I know that other people need to see because it was videos of other people having and sharing their experiences that led to me having my own experience. It's also the reason this platform exists.

The video below is my explanation of my first experience with DMT and Bufo. I go more in detail about what I experienced and how my life was affected in the weeks that followed. This video was a part of my integration which involved me verbalizing and sharing my experience as well as continuing to make sense of it all.

After sharing my experience with two of my best friends, we took a trip to Seattle and experienced Bufo together a few months later. It was a very beautiful experience that made us grow closer.

FEB 2021

My Fourth Bufo Experience

By Dorian Wallace

This is my fourth bufo experience. In this experience I released so much! It was very different from my first experience with Bufo.

No matter how many times I sit with this medicine or any other entheogen, I have to face the fear that comes up for me right before I consume it. But as soon as I'm in the experience I am free. In my second, third, and fourth Bufo experience I said the words "I remember!" Although I don't have any recollection of this, I know that entering into the experience for me is like going back to a place that I've long forgotten.

I say God a lot. This has to do with my religious upbringing and the programming associated with that. There is a disassociation between sex and God that I often purge while in these experiences because of childhood trauma and enforced religious teachings around these topics. Being taught that they are separate has created a lot of fear and shame around sex and alienation of what God truly is to me.

I apologize a lot in this experience. I apologize to my mother. I don't call her mommy, but in my experience I do. As if the child version of me is speaking. I didn't know why I said this when I came out of the experience. Not until I started integration work. I discovered a Truth that I had kept to myself for a very long time. As a child I made a decision that impacted our relationship very early in my life. I decided that I didn't want to be like my mother. Emotionally I kept my distance from her, always reminding myself that to get to close by fostering the mother/daughter relationship that she wanted, meant that I would end up just like her. I wanted to be strong, like my dad and three older brothers. I didn't want to wash dishes and fold clothes. I didn't want to always have to succumb to the provider of the household. I didn't want to "know my place". I resented her for not being an example to me of what I thought a strong black woman should be. When I realized this truth, I could not stop crying because what I also realized was that my mom was a great mother to me. She was the best mother she could be. As I thought back over my childhood I could clearly see how much she loved me and did her very best to provide for me and my brothers. About a week after the experience I called her and shared all of this with her.

Integration is just as much a part of the healing as the actual bufo experience.

The other thing that came up for me in this bufo experience that I would like to share is what was once such a source of shame and humiliation. I have facial hair, the result of PCOS, something that many women deal with. For decades I've hid this about myself and gone to such great lengths to keep this a secret. It's something that I didn't share with my ex-wife or my current partner despite our closeness and intimacy. It was my shameful secret and it impacted me daily. I wouldn't let anyone touch my face, even my partner, for fear that she would then know. I wore make-up to hide any trace of facial hair, something that I would not do otherwise. I would become anxious if someone stood too close to me. I hated it so much. It caused me so much shame, and it was on my face!

In this experience I freed myself of that shame. In hindsight, it absolutely had to be done this way and it had to be done in front of my partner or perhaps I would have walked away from the experience and never told anyone about it. Instead it led to deep and meaningful conversations around conditioning and my beliefs when it comes to standards of beauty. In that initial conversation when I shared this with my partner, I could not stop crying. The person that I worked through my integration with, shared with me that when you speak your truth for the first time, you will cry. You cry because you are releasing and hearing that truth is freeing and beautiful. Letting go of that shame was not easy, but it was healing and I am free. After sharing all of this with my partner, we've since then continued to have conversations around it. Conversations that would have been impossible for me to have before this experience.

Next I grabbed my partner and apologize some more. At some point I couldn't breath, because I could hear how her asthma was effecting her breathing, somehow her difficulty breathing became me having trouble breathing. My breath became even more shallow until it stopped and I knew that I died. But in that instance I was born. There was no fear. Just one experience melded seamlessly into the next. Death and then Birth. Then I was a never-ending being in a new form. I could see everyone around me as if for the first time. The love and acceptance I felt from them made me cry. The simplicity of this life and the beauty of it all made me sob.

So much about my experiences are personal but this is my Truth and to be able to share it with the world is what freedom is to me.
